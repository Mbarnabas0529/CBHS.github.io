Scott Pilgrim's Precious Little Life

Scott Pilgrim’s life couldn’t be more complete. At 23, he’s renting a one-room flat with his gay roommate and is constantly looking for a job—with little persistence and even less success. With a band, video games, and a high school girlfriend in his life, he’s counting down the Canadian weekdays—until the dangerously cool Ramona moves to town. > > In the whirlwind of awakening feelings, Scott is forced to face evil exes, mystical powers, and intense battles—so once again it turns out: love hurts and bursts tiles. > > Bryan Lee O’Malley’s six-part series blends the most valuable traits of Western and Eastern comics in an endlessly witty and creative way, with original humor and a remarkable story.
